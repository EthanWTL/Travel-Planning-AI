**Day 1**

### Morning (8:00 am - 12:00 pm)

* **Breakfast**: Start the day with a visit to [Fairmount Park](https://www.philadelphiazoo.org/visit/park.html) and grab breakfast at [The Original Tony Lukes](https://www.yelp.com/biz/the-original-tony-lukes-philadelphia), a popular spot for cheesesteaks. Address: 39 E Oregon Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19148
* **Accommodation**: Check-in to the [Loews Philadelphia Hotel](https://www.loewshotels.com/hotels/philadelphia/Philadelphia) located in Center City Philadelphia. Address: 1200 Market St, Philadelphia, PA 19107

### Afternoon (1:00 pm - 5:00 pm)

* **Lunch**: Head to [Pat's King of Steaks](https://www.patskingofsteaks.com/) for a classic Philly cheesesteak experience. Address: 900 Passyunk Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19147
* **Attraction**: Visit the iconic **Philadelphia Zoo**, home to over 1,300 animals from around the world. Spend the afternoon exploring the various exhibits and shows.

### Evening (6:00 pm - 10:00 pm)

* **Dinner**: Make a reservation at [Bar Ferdinand](https://www.barfernandophila.com/), a modern tapas restaurant in Center City Philadelphia. Address: 313 S 20th St, Philadelphia, PA 19103
* **Evening Stroll**: Take an evening stroll through **Fairmount Park** and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

### Nighttime (10:00 pm - 11:30 pm)

* **Nightlife**: Head to [The Stratus Rooftop Bar](https://www.stratustophotelp.com/roof-top-bar) for a drink and take in the stunning views of Philadelphia. Address: 1213 Pine St, Philadelphia, PA 19107

**Day 2**

### Morning (8:00 am - 12:00 pm)

* **Breakfast**: Grab breakfast at [The Bakeshop on 20th](https://www.thebakeshophon20.com/), a cozy bakery with delicious pastries. Address: 269 S 20th St, Philadelphia, PA 19103
* **Attraction**: Visit the famous **Reading Terminal Market**, one of the largest and oldest markets in the country. Spend some time browsing the stalls and sampling local foods.

### Afternoon (1:00 pm - 5:00 pm)

* **Lunch**: Head to [El Vez](https://www.elvezphilly.com/), a vibrant Mexican restaurant with delicious tacos and burritos. Address: 121 S 13th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107
* **Attraction**: Visit the iconic **Liberty Bell**, an historic symbol of American independence.

### Evening (6:00 pm - 10:00 pm)

* **Dinner**: Make a reservation at [Abe Fisher](https://www.abe-fisher.com/), a fine dining restaurant with a focus on seasonal ingredients. Address: 1623 Sansom St, Philadelphia, PA 19103
* **Evening Entertainment**: Catch a show or performance in the city's vibrant theater scene.

### Nighttime (10:00 pm - 11:30 pm)

* **Nightlife**: End the night with a drink at [The Royal Tavern](https://www.theroyaltavern.com/), a cozy bar with a wide selection of beers and cocktails. Address: 937 S St, Philadelphia, PA 19147

This itinerary provides a mix of popular attractions, delicious food options, and lively nightlife experiences. The Loews Philadelphia Hotel is conveniently located in Center City Philadelphia, making it easy to explore the city's main attractions. Enjoy your trip!
