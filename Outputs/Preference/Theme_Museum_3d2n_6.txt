Here is a sample trip plan for 3 days and 2 nights in Philadelphia with a museum theme:

**Day 1**

* Breakfast: **The Bakeshop on 20th** (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM) - Try their delicious pastries and coffee to start your day.
* Attraction daytime:
	+ **National Constitution Center** (9:00 AM - 10:30 AM) - Learn about the history of the US Constitution at this interactive museum.
	+ **Independence Hall** (11:00 AM - 12:30 PM) - Visit the birthplace of American democracy and see where the Declaration of Independence was signed.
* Lunch:
	+ **Reading Terminal Market** (1:00 PM - 2:00 PM) - Try a classic Philly cheesesteak or some fresh produce at this historic market.
* Attraction afternoon:
	- **Philadelphia Museum of Art** (3:00 PM - 5:00 PM) - Run up the famous "Rocky Steps" and explore one of the largest art museums in the country.
* Dinner:
	+ **Vernick Food & Drink** (7:00 PM - 8:30 PM) - Enjoy a meal at this modern American restaurant with a focus on seasonal ingredients.
* Accommodation:
	+ **The Logan Philadelphia, Curio Collection by Hilton**

**Day 2**

* Breakfast: **The Green Line Cafe** (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM) - Grab some coffee and a breakfast sandwich to go before your busy day.
* Attraction daytime:
	+ **Eastern State Penitentiary** (9:30 AM - 11:30 AM) - Explore the historic prison where Al Capone once lived.
	+ **The Franklin Institute Science Museum** (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM) - Learn about science and technology at this interactive museum.
* Lunch:
	+ **Di Bruno Bros.** (2:30 PM - 3:30 PM) - Try some Italian cuisine or grab a sandwich to go from this historic deli.
* Attraction afternoon:
	- **Franklin Square** (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM) - Visit one of the oldest public parks in the country and enjoy the beautiful gardens and fountain show.
* Dinner:
	+ **Pizzeria Vetri** (7:30 PM - 9:00 PM) - Enjoy a wood-fired pizza at this modern Italian restaurant.
* Accommodation:
	+ **The Logan Philadelphia, Curio Collection by Hilton**

**Day 3**

* Breakfast: **Spread Bagelry** (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM) - Try some freshly baked bagels and cream cheese to start your day.
* Attraction daytime:
	+ **Museum of the American Revolution** (9:30 AM - 11:30 AM) - Learn about the history of the American Revolution at this new museum.
	+ **Smith Memorial Playground & Playhouse** (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM) - Visit this historic playground and playhouse for some fun in the sun.
* Lunch:
	+ **Pizzeria Beddia** (2:30 PM - 3:30 PM) - Try some delicious pizza at this modern pizzeria.
* Attraction afternoon:
	- **The Barnes Foundation** (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM) - Visit one of the largest collections of impressionist and post-impressionist art in the world.
* Dinner:
	+ **Vetri Cucina** (7:30 PM - 9:00 PM) - Enjoy a meal at this modern Italian restaurant with a focus on seasonal ingredients.
* Accommodation:
	+ **The Logan Philadelphia, Curio Collection by Hilton**

Note that these are just sample plans and you can adjust the schedule to fit your own preferences and interests.
