Based on the provided data, I'll plan a 2-day, 1-night trip to Philadelphia with a museum theme.

**Day 1**

* **Morning**: Start at Independence Hall (attraction) and take a guided tour of the historic landmark.
	+ Time: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
	+ Address: 520 Chestnut St
	+ Price: Free admission, but tickets required for tours
* **Lunch**: Head to Reading Terminal Market (restaurant) for a food court experience with various options. Try Amish baked goods and cheese steaks.
	+ Time: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM
	+ Address: 51 N 12th St
	+ Price: $10-$20 per person
* **Afternoon**: Visit the Philadelphia Museum of Art (attraction) to see famous works by European and American artists, including Van Gogh's "Wheat Field with Cypresses."
	+ Time: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
	+ Address: 2600 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy
	+ Price: $20 admission fee (free admission on Sundays)
* **Dinner**: Dine at the nearby Square Peg restaurant, which serves farm-to-table cuisine with a focus on local ingredients.
	+ Time: 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
	+ Address: 1234 Locust St
	+ Price: $20-$30 per person
* **Evening**: Explore the Rittenhouse Square neighborhood and enjoy some live music or street performers in the park.

**Accommodations**

For accommodations, I recommend staying at the Kimpton Hotel Monaco Philadelphia (hotel) for its unique blend of boutique hotel charm and museum-like atmosphere.
	+ Address: 433 Chestnut St
	+ Price: $150-$200 per night

**Day 2**

* **Morning**: Visit the Franklin Institute Science Museum (attraction), which features interactive exhibits, a giant model train display, and a 3D theater showing educational films.
	+ Time: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
	+ Address: 222 N 20th St
	+ Price: $20 admission fee
* **Lunch**: Grab a bite to eat at the nearby Pizzeria Vetri (restaurant), which serves authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas.
	+ Time: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM
	+ Address: 1939 Callowhill St
	+ Price: $15-$25 per person
* **Afternoon**: Head to the Barnes Foundation (attraction) for an extensive collection of impressionist, post-impressionist, and modern art by artists such as Renoir, Cezanne, Matisse, and Picasso.
	+ Time: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
	+ Address: 2025 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy
	+ Price: $20 admission fee

**Additional Tips**

* Consider purchasing a Philadelphia CityPASS for discounted admission to the attractions listed above.
* Take advantage of the hotel's concierge service to book dinner reservations and arrange for transportation to and from attractions.
* If time allows, explore other museums in Philadelphia, such as the National Constitution Center or the Rodin Museum.
