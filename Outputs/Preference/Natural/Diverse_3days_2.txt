Here is a detailed travel itinerary in Philadelphia that you can follow step-by-step:

**Day 1**

*   **Breakfast:** Start your day with a delicious breakfast at "Dottie's Donuts" (4529 Springfield Ave), a vegan-friendly spot serving unique donut flavors and coffee drinks.
*   **Attractions before lunch:**
    *   Visit the iconic "Independence Hall" (520 Chestnut St), where the Declaration of Independence and the United States Constitution were signed. Take a guided tour to learn about its history.
    *   Explore the nearby "Liberty Bell Center" (6th St & Chesnut St), home to the famous Liberty Bell, an iconic symbol of American freedom.
*   **Lunch:** Head to "Pat's King of Steaks" (1237 E Passyunk Ave) for a classic Philly cheesesteak experience. Try their signature sandwich with thinly sliced steak, melted cheese, and saut√©ed onions on a crusty roll.
*   **Attractions after lunch:**
    *   Take a short walk to the "Reading Terminal Market" (51 N 12th St), one of the largest and oldest public markets in the United States. Browse through various vendors offering fresh produce, meats, baked goods, and handmade crafts.
*   **Dinner:** Enjoy dinner at "Zorba's Taverna" (2230 Fairmount Ave), a Greek restaurant serving authentic dishes like gyro, souvlaki, and moussaka. Don't forget to try their refreshing Greek salad!
*   **Accommodation:** Stay overnight at the "Hotel Monaco Philadelphia" (433 Chestnut St), a luxurious boutique hotel located in the heart of downtown Philadelphia.

**Day 2**

*   **Breakfast:** Begin your day with breakfast at "Green Eggs Cafe" (212 S 13th St), a popular spot serving creative egg dishes and sandwiches. Try their signature green eggs scrambled with herbs and spices.
*   **Attractions before lunch:**
    *   Visit the "Philadelphia Museum of Art" (2600 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy), famous for its steps from the movie Rocky. Explore the museum's vast collections, including European paintings, American art, and Asian cultural artifacts.
*   **Lunch:** Grab lunch at "Franky Bradley's" (1320 Chancellor St), an Irish pub serving classic sandwiches, salads, and soups. Don't miss their famous shepherd's pie!
*   **Attractions after lunch:**
    *   Visit the "Mutter Museum" (19 S 22nd St), a unique medical museum showcasing unusual medical specimens and artifacts. Be prepared for a quirky and fascinating experience.
    *   Explore the charming "Elfreth's Alley" (126 Elfreths Aly), America's oldest continuously inhabited street, lined with historic homes dating back to the 18th century.
    *   Visit the nearby "National Constitution Center" (525 Arch St), an educational institution dedicated to promoting a deeper understanding of the US Constitution and its significance.
*   **Dinner:** Enjoy dinner at "La Nonna" (214 S St), an Italian restaurant serving authentic dishes like pasta, risotto, and pizza. Be sure to try their house-made limoncello!
*   **Accommodation:** Stay overnight at the "DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Philadelphia Center City" (4509 Island Ave), a convenient and comfortable hotel located near several attractions.

**Day 3**

*   **Breakfast:** Start your day with breakfast at "Ristorante Aroma" (1245 3rd St), an Italian restaurant serving traditional dishes like omelets, pancakes, and sandwiches.
*   **Attractions before lunch:**
    *   Visit the historic "Independence Square Park" (200 N 6th St), a charming public park featuring historic buildings, monuments, and scenic walkways.
*   **Lunch:** Grab lunch at "Lou's Cafe" (1001 S 17th St), a cozy spot serving creative sandwiches, salads, and soups. Don't miss their delicious crab cake sandwich!
*   **Attractions after lunch:**
    *   Visit the iconic "Philadelphia Art Alliance" (251 S 18th St), a cultural institution showcasing contemporary art exhibitions, concerts, and performances.
    *   Explore the historic "Elm Park" (1700 Lombard St), a charming park featuring beautiful gardens, walking paths, and picturesque views.
*   **Dinner:** Enjoy dinner at "The Love" (130 S 18th St), a modern American restaurant serving creative dishes like burgers, salads, and sandwiches. Don't forget to try their decadent chocolate cake!
*   **Accommodation:** Stay overnight at the "Rittenhouse Hotel" (210 W Rittenhouse Sq), a luxurious boutique hotel located in the heart of downtown Philadelphia.

Enjoy your trip to Philadelphia!
