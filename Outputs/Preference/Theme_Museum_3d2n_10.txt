Here is a 3-day, 2-night travel plan to Philadelphia with a museum theme:

**Day 1**

* **Morning**: Visit the Philadelphia Museum of Art (9:00 am - 12:00 pm)
	+ Address: 2600 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy
	+ Spend a few hours exploring the impressive art collection and iconic steps from Rocky.
* **Lunch**: Head to Reading Terminal Market (12:00 pm - 1:30 pm)
	+ Address: 51 N 12th St
	+ Try some of the famous Philly cheesesteaks or explore the various food stalls.
* **Afternoon**: Visit the Franklin Institute Science Museum (2:00 pm - 5:00 pm)
	+ Address: 222 N 20th St
	+ Engage in interactive exhibits and experiments, including a giant heart and a submarine simulator.
* **Dinner**: Enjoy dinner at Distrito (6:00 pm - 8:00 pm)
	+ Address: 3945 Chestnut St
	+ Try some delicious Mexican cuisine with a modern twist.
* **Accommodation**: Stay at the Loews Philadelphia Hotel (check-in time: 3:00 pm)
	+ Address: 1200 Market St

**Day 2**

* **Morning**: Visit the National Constitution Center (9:00 am - 12:00 pm)
	+ Address: 525 Arch St
	+ Learn about the history and significance of the US Constitution.
* **Lunch**: Grab lunch at Square 1682 (12:00 pm - 1:30 pm)
	+ Address: 121 S 17th St
	+ Enjoy some contemporary American cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients.
* **Afternoon**: Visit the Museum of the American Revolution (2:00 pm - 5:00 pm)
	+ Address: 101 S 3rd St
	+ Explore the rich history of the American Revolution and its impact on modern America.
* **Dinner**: Dine at Vetri Cucina (6:00 pm - 8:00 pm)
	+ Address: 1312 Spruce St
	+ Enjoy some authentic Italian cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients.
* **Accommodation**: Stay at the Loews Philadelphia Hotel (overnight)

**Day 3**

* **Morning**: Visit the Barnes Foundation (9:00 am - 12:00 pm)
	+ Address: 2025 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy
	+ Marvel at the impressive collection of impressionist, post-impressionist, and modern art.
* **Lunch**: Grab lunch at Aqimero (12:00 pm - 1:30 pm)
	+ Address: 10 Avenue Of The Arts
	+ Enjoy some modern American cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients.
* **Afternoon**: Visit the Liberty Bell Center (2:00 pm - 5:00 pm)
	+ Address: 526 Market St
	+ Learn about the history and significance of the Liberty Bell, an iconic symbol of American freedom.

This itinerary provides a mix of cultural experiences, historical landmarks, and delicious food, with a focus on museum themes. Enjoy your trip to Philadelphia!
