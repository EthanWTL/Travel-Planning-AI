### Summary of Recommendations:
- **Accommodations:** The Rittenhouse Hotel offers excellent quality and safety in a prime location.
- **Vietnamese Restaurants:** Banh Mi Cali and Vietnam Cafe provide authentic Vietnamese cuisine.
- **Historical Attractions:** Independence Hall, Liberty Bell, National Constitution Center, Philadelphia Museum of Art, Eastern State Penitentiary, and The Betsy Ross House are must-visit historical sites.

This itinerary is designed to optimize travel routes and ensure a rich historical experience while enjoying the best of Philadelphia's culinary offerings.
