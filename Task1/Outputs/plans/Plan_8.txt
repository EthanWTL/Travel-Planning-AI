### 2-Day Food-Oriented Itinerary in Philadelphia

#### Day 1: Exploring Middle Eastern Flavors

**Morning:**
- **Breakfast at Café La Maude**  
  Address: 816 N 4th St, Philadelphia, PA 19123  
  Time: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM  
  Cuisine: Mediterranean, American (New)  
  Enjoy a delightful brunch with a mix of Mediterranean and American flavors.

**Mid-Morning:**
- **Visit Reading Terminal Market**  
  Address: 51 N 12th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107  
  Time: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM  
  Explore local vendors and grab a snack or two, including some Middle Eastern treats.

**Lunch:**
- **Lunch at Zahav**  
  Address: 237 St James Pl, Philadelphia, PA 19106  
  Time: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM  
  Cuisine: Middle Eastern, Israeli  
  Experience award-winning modern Israeli cuisine in a vibrant setting.

**Afternoon:**
- **Visit the Philadelphia Museum of Art**  
  Address: 2600 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy, Philadelphia, PA 19130  
  Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM  
  Explore the art collections and enjoy the iconic steps.

**Evening:**
- **Dinner at Suraya**  
  Address: 1528 Frankford Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125  
  Time: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM  
  Cuisine: Middle Eastern  
  Savor authentic Lebanese dishes in a beautifully designed space.

**Night:**
- **Cocktails at The Franklin Bar**  
  Address: 112 S 18th St, Philadelphia, PA 19103  
  Time: 8:30 PM - 10:00 PM  
  Enjoy craft cocktails in an intimate setting.

**Accommodation:**
- **The Rittenhouse Hotel**  
  Address: 210 W Rittenhouse Sq, Philadelphia, PA 19103  
  Stars: 4.0  
  Price: $$$  
  Known for excellent service and a prime location.
