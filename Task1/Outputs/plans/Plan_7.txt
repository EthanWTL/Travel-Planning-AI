### 2-Day Travel Itinerary for Philadelphia

#### Day 1: Historical Exploration

**Morning:**
- **Breakfast at Cafe Lift**  
  Address: 428 N 13th St, Philadelphia, PA 19123  
  Time: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM  
  Description: Enjoy a hearty breakfast with a variety of options in a cozy atmosphere.

- **Visit Independence Hall**  
  Address: 520 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19106  
  Time: 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM  
  Description: Explore the birthplace of the United States, where the Declaration of Independence and Constitution were debated and adopted.

**Lunch:**
- **Lunch at El Vez**  
  Address: 121 S 13th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107  
  Time: 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM  
  Description: A vibrant Mexican restaurant known for its delicious tacos and guacamole.

**Afternoon:**
- **Visit the Liberty Bell Center**  
  Address: 6th St & Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19106  
  Time: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM  
  Description: See the iconic symbol of American independence and learn about its history.

- **Explore the National Constitution Center**  
  Address: 525 Arch St, Philadelphia, PA 19106  
  Time: 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM  
  Description: An interactive museum dedicated to the U.S. Constitution.

**Evening:**
- **Dinner at Cantina Dos Segundos**  
  Address: 931 N 2nd St, Philadelphia, PA 19123  
  Time: 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM  
  Description: Enjoy a casual dining experience with authentic Mexican cuisine.

- **Check-in at Alexander Inn**  
  Address: 301 S 12th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107  
  Description: A comfortable stay with good service and an excellent location.
