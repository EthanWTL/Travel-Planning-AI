### 2-Day Food-Oriented Itinerary in Philadelphia

#### Day 1: Exploring Japanese Cuisine

**Morning:**
- **Breakfast at Cafe Lift**  
  Address: 428 N 13th St, Philadelphia, PA 19123  
  Time: 9:00 AM  
  Description: Start your day with a hearty breakfast at this popular café known for its delicious brunch options.  
  Price: $$

**Mid-Morning:**
- **Visit Reading Terminal Market**  
  Address: 51 N 12th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107  
  Time: 10:30 AM  
  Description: Explore this bustling market filled with local vendors. Don’t miss the chance to try some local delicacies.  
  Price: Free entry, food prices vary.

**Lunch:**
- **Lunch at Double Knot**  
  Address: 120 S 13th St, Philadelphia, PA 19107  
  Time: 12:30 PM  
  Description: Enjoy a mix of sushi and Japanese dishes in a stylish setting.  
  Price: $$$

**Afternoon:**
- **Visit the Philadelphia Museum of Art**  
  Address: 2600 Benjamin Franklin Pkwy, Philadelphia, PA 19130  
  Time: 2:30 PM  
  Description: Explore the art collections and take a photo at the famous "Rocky Steps."  
  Price: $$

**Evening:**
- **Dinner at Morimoto**  
  Address: 723 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19106  
  Time: 6:00 PM  
  Description: Experience high-end Japanese cuisine at this renowned restaurant by Iron Chef Masaharu Morimoto.  
  Price: $$$$

**Night:**
- **Stay at The Rittenhouse Hotel**  
  Address: 210 W Rittenhouse Sq, Philadelphia, PA 19103  
  Stars: 4.0  
  Price: $$$  
  Description: Enjoy luxury accommodations in a prime location with excellent service.
