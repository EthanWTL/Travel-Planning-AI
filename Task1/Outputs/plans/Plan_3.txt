### 4-Day Budget Travel Itinerary in Philadelphia

#### Day 1: Arrival and Exploring the Historic District
- **Morning:**
  - **Accommodation Check-in:** 
    - **Alexander Inn** (301 S 12th St, $$, average quality, excellent location)
  - **Breakfast:** 
    - **Cafe Lift** (428 N 13th St, $$, excellent flavor, good freshness)

- **Afternoon:**
  - **Attraction:** 
    - **Independence Hall** (520 Chestnut St, Free, historical site)
  - **Lunch:** 
    - **Banh Mi Cali** (900 Arch St, $$, Vietnamese, good freshness)

- **Evening:**
  - **Attraction:** 
    - **Liberty Bell Center** (6th St & Chestnut St, Free, historical site)
  - **Dinner:** 
    - **Jim's South St** (400 S St, $$, famous for cheesesteaks)
  - **Night Activity:** 
    - **Ghost Tour of Philadelphia** (5TH And Chestnut, $$, family-friendly)
